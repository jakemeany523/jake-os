# Viral Content Scout

Engagement opportunity monitoring across AI Twitter/X.

## What It Does

Monitors competitor accounts, AI thought leaders, and trending evaluation content to identify high-signal engagement opportunities. Finds moments worth quote-tweeting, replying to, or riffing on with original angles.

## What It Monitors

- Competitor Twitter accounts (Arize, Arena, Artificial Analysis, Braintrust, LangSmith)
- AI thought leaders and influential accounts
- Trending evaluation and benchmark discussions
- Model launch conversations with engagement potential

## Output

Curated list of engagement opportunities with suggested angles, sorted by signal quality and timeliness.
