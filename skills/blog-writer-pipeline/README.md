# Blog Writer Pipeline

End-to-end article production for the Education Hub.

## What It Does

Takes a target keyword and produces a publish-ready article through a single autonomous workflow. Chains Ahrefs MCP (keyword + SERP research), Copy Reviewer (brand voice enforcement), and graphics generation with one approval gate at the outline stage.

## Workflow

1. Keyword research via Ahrefs MCP (volume, difficulty, SERP analysis)
2. Competitive content audit (what's ranking, what angles are covered)
3. Outline generation with SEO structure
4. **Approval gate:** Outline reviewed before drafting
5. Full article draft with proper heading hierarchy
6. Copy Review pass (brand voice, banned phrases, tone)
7. SEO metadata generation (title tag, meta description, OG tags)
8. Social repurposing seeds for post-publish distribution

## Integration Points

- **Ahrefs MCP:** Keyword research, SERP analysis, competitor content audit
- **Copy Reviewer:** Brand voice enforcement on final draft
- **Content Repurposer:** Social seeds enable multi-platform distribution
