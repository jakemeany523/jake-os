# Meeting Prep Generator

CRM-powered meeting preparation briefs.

## What It Does

Auto-generates meeting prep by pulling CRM data (Pipedrive), email history, Fireflies transcripts from previous meetings, and calendar context. Outputs a 2-minute actionable brief.

## What's In a Prep Brief

- **Attendee context:** Who they are, their role, previous interactions
- **Deal status:** Current pipeline stage, deal value, last activity
- **Meeting history:** Key points from previous Fireflies transcripts
- **Talking points:** Suggested topics based on deal stage and recent context
- **Open items:** Anything unresolved from previous meetings

## Integration Points

- **Pipedrive MCP:** Deal data, contact info, activity history
- **Fireflies MCP:** Previous meeting transcripts and summaries
- **Google Calendar:** Meeting details and attendee list
