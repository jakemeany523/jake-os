# Email Rubric (LayerLens)

## Dimensions

### 1. subject_line
Specific, curiosity-gap, under 50 chars. "Newsletter #12" = 2. "Claude 3.7 just rewrote the MMLU leaderboard" = 9.

### 2. preview_text
Completes or contradicts the subject line. Not redundant. 90-110 chars sweet spot.

### 3. opening_hook
First 2 lines earn the scroll. No "hope you had a great week." Lead with the finding, the story, or the stake.

### 4. scannability
Subheads, short paragraphs, bolded key phrases. Wall of text = 3.

### 5. one_thing_rule
Does the email do ONE job (announce / educate / drive click)? Multi-CTA = 4. Single focused CTA = 9.

### 6. voice_fit
Jake first-person for personal sends; LayerLens plural for product updates. Consistent throughout.

### 7. cta_quality
Clear, specific, one primary action. "Learn more" = 3. "See the 15-model head-to-head" = 9.

## Hard caps

- Em dash = 3.0 max.
- Banned phrase = 4.0 max.
- More than one primary CTA = 5.0 max.
- Broken link or placeholder URL = 2.0 max.
