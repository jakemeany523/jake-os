# LinkedIn Education Drafter

LinkedIn-specific educational content for enterprise buyers and tech leaders.

## What It Does

Produces content tailored to LinkedIn's audience: frameworks, insights, and decision-making narratives rather than benchmark charts. Targets enterprise buyers and C-suite audiences who need to understand why AI evaluation matters without deep technical background.

## Design Decisions

- Frameworks over data dumps
- Decision-making narratives over product pitches
- Enterprise buyer language, not developer jargon
- Each post teaches a concept, not just announces a result
