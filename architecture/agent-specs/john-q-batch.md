# John Q Weekly Batch

**Schedule:** Sunday at 6:00 PM (local timezone)
**Delivery:** Workspace file
**Type:** Claude scheduled task (cron-based)

## Purpose

Generates 3-5 satirical LinkedIn posts for a parody character account. Automated content creation with strict voice and formatting rules.

## Output

Each post follows the character's established voice: satirical takes on AI industry trends, corporate jargon, and LinkedIn culture. Posts are generated with specific formatting rules, topic constraints, and tone calibration.

## Design Decisions

**Why automate parody content:** Consistent character voice is hard to maintain manually. The LLM handles voice consistency; the human handles topic selection and timing.

**Why Sunday evening:** Posts are ready for the Monday-Friday LinkedIn cycle. Reviewed Sunday night or Monday morning.
